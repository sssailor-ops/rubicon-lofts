What We Build

Our Conversions

Every loft is different. We assess your roof structure and design the right conversion type — maximising space, natural light, and value.

01
🔷
Dormer Loft Conversion

The most popular option in SW London terraces. A flat-roofed extension to the rear slope adds full headroom and a proper bedroom with ensuite. Maximum usable space with clean lines.

02
🔺
Hip-to-Gable Conversion

Ideal for semi-detached and end-of-terrace homes. We extend the hipped roof to create a vertical gable wall — dramatically increasing the footprint of your new loft space.

03
⬡
Mansard Conversion

A premium solution offering the most floor space possible. Near-vertical rear walls with a flat roof — common in Victorian and Edwardian properties throughout Wimbledon, Kingston and Raynes Park.

04
◈
Velux / Rooflight

Where planning is sensitive or budgets are tighter, Velux conversions work within the existing roof structure. Clean, fast, and often permitted development — no planning permission needed.

05
⊞
L-Shaped Dormer

Two dormers combined into one large L-shaped structure — perfect for Victorian end-of-terrace and semi-detached properties. Creates multiple rooms in a single build, including master suite, bedroom and bathroom.

06
⬒
Design & Build Package

Full service from architect drawings and planning applications through to structural engineering, building regulations, and completed handover. One point of contact throughout your entire project.

How It Works

Your project, step by step

01
Free Survey

We visit your home, assess the roof structure, discuss your goals and give you an honest feasibility assessment and indicative cost — completely free of charge.

02
Design & Planning

Our architects produce full drawings. We handle planning applications, party wall notices, and building regulations submissions. You're kept informed at every step.

03
Pre-Start Meeting

Before a single nail is driven, we hold a detailed pre-start meeting at your home. Schedule of works, material choices, scaffold arrangement, and site access — all agreed upfront.

04
Build & Handover

Our experienced team executes the build to programme. Building control sign-off, fire safety report, and a full clean handover. We don't leave until you're delighted.

A word on how we work

Rubicon Lofts was founded on a simple principle: treat every project as if it were our own home. That means being honest about what's feasible, communicating clearly throughout the build, and not walking away until the job is right.

We're a tight-knit team of experienced builders, carpenters, and project managers. We don't subcontract to strangers — the same faces you meet at your pre-start are the ones on site every day.

Most of our work comes through referrals from previous clients. We think that says more about our quality than any marketing could.

Our focus area: We deliberately limit ourselves to SW London and the KT postcode area. This keeps our teams sharp, local, and genuinely excellent in the area — rather than spread thin across the whole of London.
